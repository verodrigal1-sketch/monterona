Holy Week in Pampanga

Traditions, Faith, and Culture

Trivia: Did you know Holy Week is a time of reflection, sacrifice, and renewal.
Trivia: Did you know Meat is avoided on Fridays during Lent.
Trivia: Did you know Fasting is common on Ash Wednesday and Good Friday.
Trivia: Did you know Many people attend confession during Holy Week.
Trivia: Did you know Women discovered the empty tomb on Easter Sunday.
Trivia: Did you know Judas Iscariot betrayed Jesus during Holy Week.
Trivia: Did you know The word “Hosanna” is shouted on Palm Sunday.
Trivia: Did you know The color violet is used during most of Holy Week.
Trivia: Did you know Church bells are often silent from Thursday to Saturday.
Trivia: Did you know In Pampanga, reenactments of the crucifixion are held.
